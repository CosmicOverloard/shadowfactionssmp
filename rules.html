Server Rules

1. General Rules

  • Be respectful to all players and staff.
  • No griefing or stealing from others outside of faction conflict.
  • Do not abuse bugs or glitches.
  • Use common sense.

2. Chat Rules

  • No spamming or excessive caps.
  • No hate speech, slurs, or harassment.
  • Keep chat friendly and appropriate for all ages.
  • English only in global chat.

3. Gameplay Rules

  • Do not use X-Ray or unfair resource packs.
  • No AFK farming or auto-clickers.
  • Respect protected claims and faction land.
  • No bypassing intended game mechanics.

4. Faction Rules

  • No insiding (betraying your faction for personal gain).
  • Allies must be declared; backstabbing is not allowed.
  • Raiding is allowed, but toxicity is not.

5. Mods & Clients

  • Allowed: Optifine, minimap (no cave radar), replay mod.
  • Disallowed: KillAura, Speed, Fly hacks, etc.
  • If you're unsure, ask staff before using any mod.
